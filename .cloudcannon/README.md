ST. BEDE THE VENERABLE WEBSITE GUIDE

Quick Reference & Training Cheat Sheet

\====================================================================

Welcome to the website management guide for St. Bede the Venerable!

This site is powered by CloudCannon, which allows you to update Mass

times, announcements, and parish information easily without touching

any code.

\--------------------------------------------------------------------

1\. GETTING STARTED

\--------------------------------------------------------------------

• Login Web Address: https://app.cloudcannon.com

• Enter your assigned email address and password.

• Once logged in, click on "St. Bede Website" to open the Visual Editor.

\--------------------------------------------------------------------

2\. MAKING QUICK EDITS (VISUAL EDITOR)

\--------------------------------------------------------------------

• Updating Text:

Click directly on any text on the page (like the Pastor's message

or an event description) and type your changes.

• Sidebar Controls (Mass Ticker & Navigation):

Look at the left sidebar for special sections:

- Mass & Confession Ticker: Update top-of-page alert notices here.

- Navigation: Click "Add Item" to add a new link, or drag links up

and down to reorder them in the main menu.

\--------------------------------------------------------------------

3\. WORKING WITH IMAGES

\--------------------------------------------------------------------

• To replace a photo, hover over the image and click the "Update Image"

or "Upload" button.

• For best results, use horizontal (landscape) photos for announcements

and main banners.

\--------------------------------------------------------------------

4\. SAVING & GOING LIVE (IMPORTANT!)

\--------------------------------------------------------------------

• As you type, the preview screen updates live on your screen.

HOWEVER, changes will NOT be visible to the public until you click

the blue "Save" button in the upper right-hand corner.

• Once you click "Save," the site will automatically update online

within 1-2 minutes.

\--------------------------------------------------------------------

5\. NEED HELP OR ACCIDENTALLY MADE A MISTAKE?

\--------------------------------------------------------------------

• Undo: If you haven't saved yet, click the "Discard Changes" button

at any time to reset the page.

• Contact: If you experience any technical difficulties, reach out to

your parish web supervisor.

\====================================================================
